How to play

Potion Pop basics

  1. Drag ingredients into the cauldron, or click them for a quick toss.
  2. Match one of the recipe cards exactly. Extra or wrong ingredients ruin the brew.
  3. Successful potions add time and boost your combo bonus.
  4. Missed ingredients, failed potions, or an empty timer cost the shop dearly.

Tiny tip: watch the recipe cards first, then grab the ingredients you actually need. Chaotic soup is still soup, but it is not profitable soup.
